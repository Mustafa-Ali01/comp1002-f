The Phone Booth Model 700: Engineered for Excellence

Our phone booth isn't just a box; it's a marvel of modern telecommunication engineering. The Model 700 is designed for durability, clarity, and reliability, ensuring that wherever you are, you can make that vital connection.

Key Features of the Model 700:

Weatherproof Exterior
Constructed with reinforced aluminum and shatter-resistant glass. Our booths can withstand the harshest elements, from desert sun to snowstorms.
Soundproofing Technology
Enjoy a conversation free from outside noise. The booth's walls are lined with patented acoustic foam, providing a cone of silence for your calls.
Automatic Coin Return
Dropped a nickel but didn't connect? Our precision-engineered coin slot system automatically returns your money if the call doesn't go through. **No lost change!**
Bright, Internal Lighting
A continuously powered, energy-efficient light ensures you can dial easily, day or night. Never fumble for the number again.

Universal Communications is committed to maintaining the highest standards of public access communication. The Model 700 is a testament to that pledge.
