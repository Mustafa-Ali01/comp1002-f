Universal Communications: A History of Connection

Founded in 1948 by visionary entrepreneur Arthur Bell, Universal Communications (UC) was established with a singular goal: to ensure every citizen has access to affordable, reliable communication. We started with a few key locations in major metropolitan centers and have grown into the nation's premier provider of public telephone access.

Our Mission & Values

"To bridge the distance between people with a commitment to quality, integrity, and public service. We believe a clear connection is a cornerstone of a thriving community."

Our corporate values center on **Accessibility**, **Reliability**, and **Innovation**. We are constantly refining the Model 700 design to improve durability and user experience, and we are proud to employ over 10,000 service technicians nationwide.
